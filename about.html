About us

History and future plan

  • 1961: Antarctican Treaty was signed. Flights were taken place in Antarctica for the first time
  • 1981: Antarctican felt flights to and from Antarctica were too infrequent, disorganised, and expensive. The airline is estabished to resolved the problem
  • 2001: Antarctica Airlines reached 5 destinations
  • 2011: Antarctica Airlines is voted as "Airline with highest rate of on-time"
  • 2021: Despite general aviation difficulties, Antarctica Airlines still recorded profit
  • 2023: Antarctica Airlines started to present in digital platform. First start is website
  • 2041: Antarctica Airlines will reach 100 interntional destinations.
  • 2061: Superjet will be expected start flying. Flight from South Pole City to North Pole Island will be shorten to 6 hours
